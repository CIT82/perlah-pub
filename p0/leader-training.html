Program Outline

What we cover in the series

Grounded in lived experience, these sessions turn leadership concepts into skills you can use right away.

1

Foundation of Leadership

Set the tone with presence, example, and a growth mindset (consistency > intensity).

  • Your "why" story
  • Personal standards & habits
  • Leading by example
2

People Over Process

Build psychological safety and trust. Know your team and coach.

  • Team trust & culture
  • Feedback & rewards
  • Mentorship vs. micromanagement
3

Real-World Challenges

Delegate clearly, hold the line, and address conflict with respect and clarity.

  • Delegation & accountability
  • Public praise, private correction
  • Hard conversations
4

Emotional Intelligence

Lead with empathy and presence. Communicate professionally and keep morale high.

  • Giving & receiving feedback
  • Reading the room
  • Confidence without ego
5

Teaching Leadership

Develop others. Create opportunities and systems that live on when you’re not in the room.

  • Coaching new leaders
  • Leading huddles & initiatives
  • Culture that sustains
6

Leading Through Change

Equip yourself to handle uncertainty, adapt quickly, and help others stay grounded during transitions.

  • Communicating change with clarity
  • Helping teams manage stress
  • Turning obstacles into opportunities
